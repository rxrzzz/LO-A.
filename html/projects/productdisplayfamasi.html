Design Playbook (Famasi)

The Challenge:

At a point during my employment at Famasi, I became the only Graphic designer on the team, other team members relied on me for designs on things like individual presentations. These requests became very overwhelming and affected timely delivery of tasks.

The Solution:

To solve this problem, I came up with the idea of creating a playbook on Canva that contained templates of the most commonly requested designs. This way individuals on the team could come up with work (presentation slides, social media post cards) that were consistent with our brand guidelines.
