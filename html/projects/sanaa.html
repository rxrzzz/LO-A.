LO-A. Projects

Sanaa

Sanaa Beauty Limited is a beauty brand designed for various African skin and hair types. The goal with this project was to create a brand that represents the beauty and pride of the African woman and could redefine beauty standards for African women. The Sanaa logo is a wordmark logo (Gt Super Display font) with opulent curves. It represents the elegance, expressiveness and beauty of African women.
