Brand Guidelines (Famasi)

Being a startup, having a strong brand identity was a top priority to the company. I collaborated with another designer on the company logo, the Logo is a wordmark with a serif font that communicated trust and genuine services. The subtle blue color represents the purity and originality of Medicine procured from Famasi. The whole logo represents the fact that Famasi is a trust-able source for genuine, pure medication and reliable health management services. I designed a brand guide that contains the brand colours, proper uses of the logo on different backgrounds and mistakes to avoid while using the logo.
