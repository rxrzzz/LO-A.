Edo State Poverty Analysis

The Challenge:

The Edo State Federal Ministry of Humanitarian Affairs, Disaster Management and Social Development had analysed multiple datasets about poverty in the state. They wanted to present the information in a concise yet visual format. The design team at Redwire was contacted to help with this effort. While viewing the analyses, we realised that we could present the information using well designed infographics. I worked with a team of two other designers to select appropriate colours, determine the best methods of visualising the data while paying attention to readability and conciseness.

The Result:

We were able to condense a 70 page report into a concise 8 page infographic that is now an important part of the Edo Governmentâ€™s Corporate Brochure.
