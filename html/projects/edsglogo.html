Edo State Government Logo

The Challenge:

The election of a new state Governor for Edo State, Nigeria in 2020 called for a new unique logo that captures the basic historical elements, heritage and industries in Edo. Redwire bid to design such a logo in 2021 and I was handed the responsibility of designing with one of the logo options. A major historical element in Edo State is the IDIA MASK, it belonged to Idia, the mother of Oba Esigie who ruled in the 16th century and is one of the most recognisable symbols of African art in the world. The Idia mask is decorated with heads representing the Portuguese, symbolizing the Edo Empireâ€™s alliance with and influence over people from all over the world. Using this as a precedent, this logo option captures the history of Edo state and a modern typeface which represents the dawn of a new Edo.

The Result:

My design was presented to the state Governor and was the second choice in over 40 options presented by 6 competing organisations.
